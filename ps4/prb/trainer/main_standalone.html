Instructions
1: Load HEN, GoldHEN or MIRA from the host menu of your choice.

2: Load the WebRTE(PS4Trainer) payload from the host menu of your choice.

3: Launch a game on the PS4 then open the Offline Trainer Menu.

4: If any trainers are available for your game they will automatically be shown when you open the trainer menu.

5: Click the trainer that matches the update version of your game and toggle the various cheat options ON or OFF.

• TIP!  To find the update version of your game: Highlight the game on the PS4 main menu, press the options
  button and select 'Information'.

• IMPORTANT!  If you usually run your PS4 totally offline, make sure to run the WebRTE payload 'AFTER' you are
  disconnected from the internet. e.g Do Not run the WebRTE payload and then disconnect from the internet!
